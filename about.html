About Me


Hi, my name is RP!

I am currently in the dual degree masters program at Pratt, studying information science and the history of art and design. I was born in Baltimore, grew up mostly in Colorado, and moved to NYC when I was 19 where I finished undergrad at School of Visual Arts. After graduating I stayed to develop an art career and to pay the bills in between I worked in artist's studios, woodshops, at PS1, bartended, did some freelance art handling... you name it, I did it. I moved to Detroit in 2015 to get my MFA from Cranbrook in painting and stayed until September 2025 working as a professional artist and curator, as a library assistant at the Cranbrook Academy of Art Library, and as an adjunct professor teaching painting and drawing at a few colleges and universities in Detroit and the metro area.
