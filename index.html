About Me



Welcome!

This guide brings together a selection of works in queer theory and queer cinema, highlighting some of the most engaging and influential voices across both writing and film. It features my top five picks for queer cinema theory books--texts that help shape conversations around identity, power, and representation--along with five films that tell compelling queer stories in a range of styles and perspectives. It also includes a non-exhaustive list of queer directors, many who have films available through the Pratt Library, making it easy to dive in and start watching. Altogether, this guide is meant to be both a helpful starting point and an open invitation to explore, learn, and discover more. Enjoy!
